# Online Sales Growth

**Description:** Scale Saif Jewelers' Shopify store from ~$50-60K/year toward a global online business.

**Status:** Active

## Goals
- Grow monthly Shopify revenue consistently
- Build systems to convert social media followers into online buyers
- Eventually sell globally, not just locally

## Key Challenges
- Currently most sales are walk-in / in-store
- No structured funnel from social media to online purchase yet
- DM volume is high but conversion is manual and time-consuming

## Key Dates / Deadlines
- No hard deadlines — ongoing growth initiative
- Review progress at start of Q2 2026 (April)
