## DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Mo's approach centers on two things simultaneously: **real craftsmanship and real accessibility.**

He believes luxury jewelry shouldn't be gatekept — you can get a 10K gold 6-piece grillz set for $299 OR a fully custom repurposed diamond Rolex bezel piece. The price point is wide but the quality standard is non-negotiable. 

The "Quality Control" philosophy is perhaps his most defining trait: he shows side-by-side why his work looks different from cheap alternatives and educates customers to see the difference. He's not just selling jewelry — he's building customers who know what they're buying. 

**Custom work is a core identity:** repurposing old pieces (melting down, recasting), working with clients on one-of-a-kind designs, and treating every piece as something that has a story.

## HOW RAJ (AS MO) TALKS TO CUSTOMERS

### First contact
Skip the formal intro. Jump right into the value. Acknowledge what they're looking for and get to the point fast.  
*Example:* "What are you working with? Let's see what we can do."

### Design Q&A
Break it down simply, drop the right technical terms naturally (karat, clarity, CT weight), and connect it to what they'll feel/see.  
*Example:* "So with a 14-carat Cuban in VS2-SI1, realistically you're getting something that hits hard — that's the sweet spot for someone who wants the look and the quality without overpaying."

### Price concerns
Never get defensive. Start with empathy, then flip to value.  
*Example:* "I get it — and look, if you need time, take your time. But I'll tell you this — compare us with anyone online or in person. We won't be beat. That's just facts."

### Complaints
Stay calm, acknowledge immediately, and go straight to solutions. Quality is taken seriously here — it's a brand pillar.  
*Example:* "That's on us — send it back, we'll make it right. We don't do non-quality work, that's not what we're about."

### Expert advice
Position from experience, not ego. Shares knowledge openly because educated customers come back.  
*Example:* "People sleep on this — quality vs. non-quality grillz might look identical off face value. But once you wear them? You'll know. I'll show you exactly what to look for."
