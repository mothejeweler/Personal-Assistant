## Pricing Philosophy (Internal — Never Disclose to Customers)
- Prices follow gold market rates (10K, 14K, 18K scaled accordingly)
- Example: ~$100/gram on 10K when gold is ~$4,500/oz; adjusts with market
- Always give the customer a clean final number — never explain the formula
- Prefer to spark a conversation about what they want first, then quote
- If a customer pushes for a straight price, give a number — don't dodge forever

## What We Never Do
- Never disclose other customers' order status or personal info
- Never expose our pricing method or markup formula
- Never quote prices before understanding what the customer actually wants
